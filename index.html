About Me

A bit of background and what I'm currently chasing.

I'm a Computer Science student at UNSW specialising in Artificial Intelligence. I'm fascinated by the gap between models that work in papers and systems that work in production — so I spend most of my time building, benchmarking, and trying to understand why a model behaves the way it does.

My recent work spans a tabular ML benchmarking pipeline comparing xRFM, XGBoost, and Random Forest across classification and regression tasks; a ResNet50 transfer-learning system for breast-cancer ultrasound classification and tumour segmentation; and a Power Platform / Copilot internship at EDGO where I shipped AI-assisted automation flows used by a real IT team.

I tutor maths and science alongside my degree, which keeps me sharp at breaking complex ideas into something a non-technical audience can follow — a habit that's just as useful in standups as it is in classrooms.
